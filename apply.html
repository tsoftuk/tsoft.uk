Application Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in joining TSoft! When preparing your application, please ensure that you include the following:

  • Information about you: Provide an updated paragraph all about you including your location, background and passions.
  • Cover Letter: Include a brief cover letter explaining why you are a good fit for TSoft and your career goals.
  • Portfolio (if applicable): If applying for a technical or design role, please include links to examples of your work.
  • References: Include at least two professional references with contact information.

Once you have all the documents ready, please send your application via email.

Please note there is no payment for any jobs. TSoft software is fully open-source.

If you have any questions or to submit your application, please contact us at:
